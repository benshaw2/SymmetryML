Continuous symmetry
discovery & enforcement

symdisc discovers and enforces continuous symmetries using vector field infinitesimal generators. Use it to discover continuous symmetry in data and differentiable models, or to enforce continuous symmetry in model training. Both invariance and equivariance are supported.

Read the Docs NeurIPS Paper
Concept sketch: a time-dependent vector field infinitesimal generator

Symmetry Discovery

Estimate vector fields that generate group actions, either from models or data.

Application‑ready enforcement

Plug-in regularizers to encourage invariance or equivariance, keeping your existing models and training loops.

Level Set Estimation

Level‑set estimation with projected geodesic / second‑order distances for geometry-aware workflows.

Designed for scale

Batch‑friendly, differentiable components and schedules that slot into modern PyTorch training pipelines.

Where symdisc helps

  • Peek into the Blackbox: discovered symmetries can inform expected model behavior
  • Generalize to unseen data: soft (regularization) or hard (canonicalization) enforcement without augmentation.
  • Scientific ML: encode conservation laws and symmetries as generators.
